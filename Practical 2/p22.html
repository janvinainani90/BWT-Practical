About Computer

A computer is a powerful electronic device that takes in data, processes it based on programmed instructions, and produces meaningful output, like text or images, while also storing information for later use. It can perform a vast range of tasks, from writing documents and sending emails to complex calculations and accessing the internet, making it an indispensable tool in fields such as healthcare, education, and business. Modern computers are programmable, allowing them to adapt todifferent tasks by executing different sets of instructions, known as software programs.
